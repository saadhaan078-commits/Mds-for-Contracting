OVERVIEW

Structural Repair for Long-Term Building Safety

Concrete and foundation problems, if left untreated, can lead to serious structural risk. Our team specializes in foundation underpinning, concrete crack injection, and structural strengthening for older buildings, damaged slabs, and settling foundations.

We assess the root cause of the damage — whether it's soil movement, water intrusion, or material fatigue — and apply the correct repair method to restore structural integrity and prevent the problem from returning.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Concrete & Foundation Repair — Common Questions

1. How do I know if my building needs foundation repair?
Signs include visible cracks in walls or floors, doors and windows that stick, uneven floors, or gaps around window frames. A free inspection can confirm the cause.
2. What is crack injection and how does it work?
Crack injection involves filling structural cracks with epoxy or polyurethane resin under pressure, sealing the crack and restoring structural strength.
3. Is foundation underpinning disruptive to the building?
We use methods that minimize disruption, and our team plans the work in phases to keep the impact on daily use as low as possible.
4. Do you provide a warranty on structural repairs?
Yes, all structural repair work comes with a written warranty, and we conduct a full quality inspection before handover.
