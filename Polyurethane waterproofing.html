OVERVIEW

Chemical & Abrasion Resistant Coating for Exposed Decks

Polyurethane (PU) waterproofing is a highly elastic liquid-applied membrane known for its exceptional flexibility, UV stability, and resistance to chemicals and abrasion. It's the preferred system for surfaces that face constant physical wear — car park decks, balconies, walkways, and podium areas.

Because it can stretch significantly without cracking, polyurethane coatings hold up well under Saudi Arabia's temperature swings and are resistant to oil, fuel spills, and heavy vehicle traffic.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Polyurethane Waterproofing — Common Questions

1. Why choose polyurethane over other waterproofing systems?
Polyurethane offers superior elasticity and chemical resistance, making it ideal for surfaces exposed to vehicle traffic, oils, and constant movement.
2. Is it safe for pedestrian and vehicle traffic?
Yes, once fully cured, PU coatings are highly durable and specifically designed for both foot and vehicle traffic areas.
3. How long before the coating is ready for use?
Curing typically takes 24-48 hours depending on weather conditions, after which the surface is ready for full use.
4. Does it resist Saudi Arabia's high temperatures?
Yes, our UV-stable polyurethane formulas are specifically selected to perform well under intense heat and sun exposure.
