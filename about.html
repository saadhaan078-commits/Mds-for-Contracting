FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

Everything you need to know about waterproofing and construction services in Saudi Arabia.

1. What is waterproofing and why is it important?
Waterproofing is the process of making a structure resistant to water penetration. It's crucial for protecting buildings from moisture damage, preventing mold growth, avoiding structural deterioration, and extending the lifespan of your property.
2. How long does waterproofing last?
With MDS Contracting, all our waterproofing work comes with a 5-year written warranty. Depending on the type of material used, liquid waterproofing can last 5-10 years, cementitious systems 10-15 years, and bituminous membranes 15-20 years or more.
3. What types of waterproofing services do you offer?
We offer comprehensive waterproofing solutions including: Liquid Waterproofing, Cementitious Waterproofing, Bituminous Membrane, Polyurethane Membrane, Roof Waterproofing, Basement Waterproofing, Terrace Waterproofing, Bathroom Waterproofing, and Heat Proofing Coatings.
4. What signs indicate my building needs waterproofing?
Common signs include: water stains on ceilings or walls, peeling paint or wallpaper, damp or musty odors, visible mold or mildew, cracks in walls or foundation, water seepage during rain, and increased humidity levels inside the building.
5. What factors affect waterproofing costs?
Waterproofing costs depend on several factors: the size of the area, type of waterproofing material used, condition of the existing surface, accessibility of the site, complexity of the project, and warranty requirements. Contact us for a free site inspection and quote.
6. When is the best time for waterproofing in Saudi Arabia?
While waterproofing can be done year-round in Saudi Arabia, the best time is during the cooler months (October to March) when temperatures are moderate. Our advanced materials allow us to work effectively in all weather conditions.
7. Do you provide warranty on your work?
Yes! All our waterproofing work comes with a comprehensive 5-year written warranty. We stand behind our quality and offer after-sales support to ensure your complete peace of mind.
