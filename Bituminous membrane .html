OVERVIEW

Heavy-Duty Waterproofing for Industrial & Commercial Roofs

Bituminous membrane waterproofing uses torch-applied bitumen sheets that fuse directly onto the roof surface, creating an extremely durable, heavy-duty barrier against water. This system is engineered for large industrial and commercial rooftops that experience heavy foot traffic, machinery loads, or constant sun exposure.

With a lifespan of 15-20 years, bituminous membranes are the go-to choice for factories, warehouses, and commercial buildings across Saudi Arabia looking for long-term, low-maintenance waterproofing.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Bituminous Membrane — Common Questions

1. What makes bituminous membrane so durable?
The torch-applied sheets fuse directly to the roof surface, creating a thick, seamless bond that resists tearing, UV damage, and heavy traffic.
2. Is it suitable for residential roofs?
It is primarily designed for industrial and commercial roofs, though it can be used on large villa rooftops needing heavy-duty protection.
3. How long does installation take?
Depending on roof size, installation typically takes 2-5 days including surface preparation and quality checks.
4. Does it require special safety precautions?
Yes, since it involves an open flame during application, our certified team follows strict fire-safety protocols throughout the job.
